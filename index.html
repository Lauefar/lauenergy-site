Who we are

We are a small, focused team that thrives on solving complex problems. We combine long-term experience from Nordic power markets with a hands-on engineering mindset — we like building the tools, not just the slides.

Our work spans market analysis, forecasting, optimisation, and practical trading operations. We are especially strong in turning flexibility into market value — from thermal units and hydropower to wind, batteries and other flexible assets.

We’re intentionally starting small. Think “basement workshop with serious ambitions” — but with disciplined risk management and respect for market rules.
